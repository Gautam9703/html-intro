Who is Hanuman?

Hanuman is a Hindu god whose form is half-monkey, half-human. He is the most devoted follower of Prince Rama and appears in the Ramayana and many other sacred stories. Hanuman is known for his immense strength, loyalty, and courage, and he uses his powers to help Rama rescue his wife Sita.

Who is Hanuman’s Son?

Hanuman's son is Makardhwaja, born from a drop of Hanuman's sweat that fell into the ocean and was swallowed by a fish. Raised by the demon king Ahiravan, Makardhwaja became the gatekeeper of the underworld, known as Patala Lok.
